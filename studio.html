section / overview

the room.

the take.

Remote drum sessions from a small, well-treated room in Berlin — booked by producers and self-releasing artists. Every track is engineered, edited and mixed-ready before it leaves the building.

Local dates in Berlin and Brandenburg on request. Happy to come to your room with a chart and professional attitude.

section / workflow

how it
works.

Three steps, no surprises. Most tracks turn around inside 48 hours of receiving the stems.

  1. 01

    deliverables

    You send a rough mix, a tempo map (or click), and any reference tracks. I send back individual close mics, room pair, overheads, and a mixed drum stem. Phase-aligned, edited, ready to drop in.

  2. 02

    revisions

    One round of revisions included on every track — feel, fills, dynamics, alternate takes. Further passes by negotiation. Direction notes welcome; "play whatever you feel" also fine.

  3. 03

    formats

    24-bit / 48 kHz WAV by default. 96 kHz on request. Stems delivered via DropMeFiles, Dropbox, or your label's portal. MIDI stems available alongside audio for hybrid productions.
